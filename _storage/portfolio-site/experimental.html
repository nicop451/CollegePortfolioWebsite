HorizonNet.

05 Physically‑based rendering

Path tracer.

Millions of rays per frame, evaluating reflection, refraction, and absorption with the Fresnel equations — all on the GPU. Below: the rendering equation it implements, an output frame, and a snippet of the kernel.

\[ L_o(\mathbf{x}, \omega_o) = L_e(\mathbf{x}, \omega_o) + \int_{\Omega} f_r(\mathbf{x}, \omega_i, \omega_o)\, L_i(\mathbf{x}, \omega_i)\, \left|\cos(\theta_i)\right|\, d\omega_i \]
06 LLM tooling

Deal finder.

Scrapes marketplace listings and feeds them through an LLM that estimates each item's likely value from its title — then surfaces anything trading well below that estimate.

07 Mandelbrot plotter

Infinite zoom.

$z_{n+1} = z_n^2 + c$ — a deceptively simple iteration that never repeats no matter how far you zoom. Runs in parallel on the GPU for high‑framerate exploration.

08 3D modelling · paid

Paid modelling work for an NFT video game — character accessories, primarily hats. Below: the in‑game home screen and store featuring my designs.

09 Physics simulations

Tiny worlds.

AP Physics concepts turned into interactive simulations: a cloth solver, an N‑body gravity sim, and a Verlet particle system with sparse‑grid collision optimization.
